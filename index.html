About

The Tang Gang


The Citrus Kings are an adult travel roller hockey club. They compete in premier roller hockey tournaments throughout the Southeastern US. Citrus Kings also hold many events within the local Jacksonville Community. Over the years the Citrus Kings have evolved into a complete hockey program with 5 teams and rather large talent pool. The Citrus Kings will continue to grow and cultivate a postive hockey culture.
